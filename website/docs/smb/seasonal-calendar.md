# Seasonal Marketing Calendar

Month-by-month content hooks for the webmaster agent. Read `BUSINESS_TYPE` from `.site-config`, scan the current month and next month, and surface 3–5 relevant hooks to the owner.

## How to use

1. Read the owner's `BUSINESS_TYPE` (may be comma-separated for multi-mode businesses).
2. Find the current month and the next month in this file.
3. Include all `types: all` hooks plus any that match the owner's business type(s).
4. Check lead times — if a hook two months out has a long lead time, surface it now.
5. Present hooks as content suggestions with the actionable description. Cross-reference with the "Content ideas" section in the owner's SMB file for more specific angles.

## Lead time guidance

Content published the day of a holiday is too late. Customers search and plan ahead.

| Hook type | Start creating | Publish by |
|---|---|---|
| Gift-giving holidays (Valentine's, Mother's Day, Christmas) | 4 weeks before | 3 weeks before |
| Booking-dependent (weddings, travel, events) | 8 weeks before | 6 weeks before |
| Awareness months | Week before the month | First week of the month |
| Seasonal tips (winterize, spring prep) | 3 weeks before season | 2 weeks before |
| Sale events (Presidents' Day, Black Friday) | 2 weeks before | 1 week before |
| Everything else | 2 weeks before | 1 week before |

When an individual hook says "Lead time: X" it overrides these defaults.

## January

### Universal hooks

- **New Year's Day** (Jan 1) — types: all — New year hours, fresh start messaging, "what's new this year" announcements. Good time for a year-ahead preview post.
- **Martin Luther King Jr. Day** (3rd Monday) — types: all — Community service, local events, respectful acknowledgment. Not a sales hook.
- **New Year resolutions** — types: all — How your business helps people with their goals. Fitness, health, financial, organizational, creative.
- **Winter weather** — types: all — Updated hours during storms, safety tips, "we're open" reassurance posts.

### Industry hooks

- **Resolution season** — types: fitness, healthcare, salon, cleaning — New member specials, wellness packages, "new year, new you" without being cliché. Post the first week of January while motivation is high.
- **Dry January** — types: brewery, restaurant — Non-alcoholic options, mocktail menus, NA beer spotlights.
- **Tax prep season begins** — types: accounting, credit-union, insurance — "Get organized" checklists, document gathering reminders, appointment availability. W-2s and 1099s arriving.
- **Winter clearance** — types: retail, bookshop, hardware, grocery — Post-holiday sales, inventory clearance, gift card redemption reminders.
- **Wedding booking season starts** — types: florist, photography, hospitality, brewery, community-theater — Engaged couples from the holidays start booking vendors. Portfolio posts, "book your date" CTAs, venue tours. Lead time: begin now, peak through March.
- **CSA pre-season teasers** — types: farm — What's planned for the growing season, early-bird pricing previews, pickup location scouting. Lead time: sign-ups open in Feb, tease now.
- **Winter maintenance** — types: trades, hardware — Frozen pipe prevention, furnace maintenance, ice dam warnings, generator tips.
- **Year-end financial review** — types: credit-union, insurance — Rate comparisons, account optimization, insurance coverage review. "Start the year with a financial checkup."
- **Winter reading lists** — types: bookshop — Staff picks for the new year, reading challenges, book club launches.
- **Holiday pet intake** — types: animal-shelter — Post-holiday surrender season. Adoption drives, "commitment not a gift" messaging, volunteer needs.
- **Winter camping** — types: campground — For year-round parks: winter site availability, cold-weather camping tips, off-season rates.

## International holidays

Anglesite is US-primary, but businesses may serve international customers or be located outside the US. Surface these if the owner's context warrants it.

### Multi-country

- **New Year's Day** (Jan 1) — Nearly universal.
- **Easter** (date varies, Mar/Apr) — US, UK, EU, AU, CA. Retail, restaurant, florist, house-of-worship.
- **Christmas** (Dec 25) — US, UK, EU, AU, CA.
- **Mother's Day** — US/CA/AU: 2nd Sunday May. UK: 4th Sunday of Lent (Mar). Check the owner's location.
- **Father's Day** — US/CA/UK: 3rd Sunday Jun. AU: 1st Sunday Sep.

### Canada

- **Family Day** (3rd Mon Feb, most provinces) — Retail, hospitality, fitness.
- **Victoria Day** (Mon before May 25) — Long weekend, start of summer. Retail, hospitality, campground.
- **Canada Day** (Jul 1) — Equivalent to July 4th. All types.
- **Thanksgiving** (2nd Mon Oct) — Not November. Restaurant, farm, grocery.
- **Boxing Day** (Dec 26) — Major retail sales event.

### United Kingdom

- **Pancake Day / Shrove Tuesday** (date varies, Feb/Mar) — Restaurant, grocery.
- **Bank holidays** — Early May, Late May, Late August. Retail, hospitality, trades.
- **Bonfire Night / Guy Fawkes** (Nov 5) — Events, hospitality.
- **Boxing Day** (Dec 26) — Retail.

### Australia

- **Australia Day** (Jan 26) — Events, hospitality, all types.
- **ANZAC Day** (Apr 25) — Community, government, house-of-worship.
- **Melbourne Cup Day** (1st Tue Nov) — Cultural event, hospitality.
- **Boxing Day** (Dec 26) — Retail.

### EU (general)

- **Carnival / Mardi Gras** (date varies, Feb) — Restaurant, hospitality, brewery. Especially DE, NL, BE, IT.
- **May Day / Labour Day** (May 1) — Many EU countries. Closures, retail.
- **National days** vary by country — out of scope for this file, but the agent can research the specific country if needed.
